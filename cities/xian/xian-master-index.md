# Xi'an (西安) — Reference Index

## Articles & Guides

- **[adventures-around-asia-xian-things-to-do.md](adventures-around-asia-xian-things-to-do.md)** — "10 Things to Do in Xi'an" by Richelle (adventuresaroundasia.com). Muslim Market, Great Mosque, city wall cycling, Drum & Bell Towers, calligraphy classes, street food (roujiamo, biangbiang noodles), Hua Shan plank walk, Shaanxi History Museum, Terracotta Warriors.

- **[chinahighlights-terracotta-warriors-guide.md](chinahighlights-terracotta-warriors-guide.md)** — "How to Visit the Terracotta Warriors" (chinahighlights.com). All three pits, hours (8:30am–5pm), tickets (~120 RMB), Bus 306, crowd-avoidance by season/time, on-site guides (100–200 RMB), scam warnings, Huaqing Hot Springs combination.

- **[imstillhungry-muslim-quarter-food-guide.md](imstillhungry-muslim-quarter-food-guide.md)** — "50 Must-Eat Street Foods of Xi'an's Muslim Quarter" (imstillhungry.net). 50 dishes with Chinese names and prices: yang rou pao mo (30–55 RMB), roujiamo (5–8 RMB), biangbiang noodles, liang pi, soup dumplings, lamb skewers, persimmon cakes. Hui cultural context, vegetarian options.

- **[chinahighlights-city-wall-cycling-guide.md](chinahighlights-city-wall-cycling-guide.md)** — "Xi'an Ancient City Wall: Cycling & History" (chinahighlights.com). Tang to Ming history, 14km circumference, 12m high, four gates, cycling (1.5hr circuit, ~45 RMB/3hr), ticket (54 RMB), Spring Festival lanterns, Hanguang Gate Relic Museum.

- **[novo-monde-huashan-hiking-guide.md](novo-monde-huashan-hiking-guide.md)** — "Hiking Mount Huashan" (novo-monde.com). Five peaks, two routes (full circuit 13.2km/8.5hr vs cable car 4.3km/4–5hr), Cliffside Plank Walk (100m, harness 30 RMB), cable car prices (North 80 RMB, West 140 RMB), HSR from Xi'an North (30 min).

- **[chinafootprints-xian-first-timer-guide.md](chinafootprints-xian-first-timer-guide.md)** — "Xi'an First-Time Visitor Guide" (chinafootprints.com). 4–5 day itinerary: Muslim Quarter, city wall, Shaanxi History Museum, Wild Goose Pagodas, Tang Paradise, Terracotta Warriors, Huaqing Palace, art districts, Huashan day trip. Metro system (11 lines), food guide, seasonal advice.
