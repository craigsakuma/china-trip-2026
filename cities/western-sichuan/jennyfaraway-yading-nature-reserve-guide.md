# Yading Nature Reserve (亚丁自然保护区) — Hiking & Visiting Guide

**Source:** Jenny Far Away (jennyfaraway.com), independent travel blog. First-hand hiking guide with detailed logistics, altitude information, costs, and honest assessment of facilities at "The Last Pure Land on Earth."

## Quick Facts

- **Location:** Daocheng County (稻城县), Garze Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture (甘孜州), Western Sichuan.
- **Nicknames:** "Last Pure Land on Earth," "Soul of Shangri-La," "Last Shangri-La."
- **Elevation range:** 3,800 m (park entrance) to 4,700 m (Five Colour Lake).
- **Three sacred mountains:** Chenrezig/Xiannairi (仙乃日, 6,032 m), Jambeyang/Yangmaiyong (央迈勇, 5,958 m), Chanadorje/Xianuoduoji (夏诺多吉, 5,958 m).
- **Best time:** Late May–early June (spring flowers) and September–October (autumn colors, clear skies).
- **Duration:** 1–2 full days inside the park.

## Getting There

**Access route:** Litang (理塘) or Shangri-La Town/Zhongdian (中甸) → Daocheng (稻城) → Shangri-La Town/Aden (香格里拉镇/亚丁) → Ticket office → Bus to Yading Village → Park entrance.

**Transport costs:**
- Litang to Yading via Daocheng: 80 RMB shared minivan.
- Daocheng to Shangri-La Town: 1.5–2 hours, 50 RMB.
- Shangri-La Town to ticket office: 2 km, 30-min walk or 10 RMB taxi.
- **Ticket office to park entrance:** 1-hour bus ride (included in ticket).
- First bus: 7:30 AM. Last return bus: 6:30 PM.

**Daocheng Yading Airport (稻城亚丁机场):** Elevation 4,411 m — one of the world's highest airports. High altitude sickness risk immediately upon landing. Flights from Chengdu (~1 hour) but driving from Chengdu (2 days via Kangding) is safer for acclimatization.

## Hiking Routes & Times

### Short Loop (Popular, Half-Day)

1. Park entrance → Chonggu Meadow: 15–20 minutes.
2. Chonggu Meadow → Pearl Lake (珍珠海): 45 min to 1 hour (steep sections). Views of Mount Xiannairi.
3. Return same way.

### Long Loop (Full Day, Strenuous)

1. Chonggu Meadow → Luorong Pasture: 6 km, 2–3 hours. OR take electric cart (80 RMB return).
2. Luorong Pasture → Milk Lake (牛奶海): 2–2.5 hours (steep sections, challenging at altitude).
3. Milk Lake → Five Colour Lake (五色海): 30–45 minutes (steep uphill).
4. Return same way to Luorong Pasture.

### Kora Path (Full Circuit)

~12 hours over 2 days, requires guide. Sacred pilgrimage route circling the three mountains. Camping required (regulations unclear for foreigners).

## Three Sacred Mountains

The three peaks are worshipped by local Tibetans as symbols of compassion (Chenrezig), wisdom (Jambeyang), and power (Chanadorje). They tower over forested slopes, glacial lakes, waterfalls, and mountain streams. Visible from different points throughout the reserve.

## Key Attractions

- **Pearl Lake (珍珠海):** Calm, reflective lake with Xiannairi mountain backdrop. Most accessible scenic spot.
- **Milk Lake (牛奶海):** Turquoise glacial lake at 4,600 m. Named for its milky-white rim. Challenging but rewarding hike.
- **Five Colour Lake (五色海):** Highest accessible lake at 4,700 m. Shifts between blue, green, purple, and gold depending on light.
- **Luorong Pasture (洛绒牛场):** Alpine meadow with grazing yaks and mountain views. Flat, easy walking.
- **Chonggu Meadow (冲古草甸):** Gateway meadow with streams and bridges. Starting point for all routes.

## Accommodation

**Shangri-La Town (Aden):**
- YHA hostel: 25 RMB dorm beds, friendly staff, baggage storage.
- Multiple guesthouses and small hotels.
- Chinese and Tibetan restaurants; small shops for supplies.

**Yading Village (亚丁村):**
- 3 km from park entrance. Several guesthouses.
- ~120 RMB for basic twin room (shared squat toilets, hot showers available).
- Guesthouse meals available. Limited restaurant options.

**Inside the Park:**
- No official accommodation. Small shop at Chonggu Meadow sells pot noodles and snacks.
- Toilets only at ticket office, Chonggu Meadow, and Luorong Pasture.

## Practical Tips

- **Altitude is the main challenge.** Even acclimated visitors find the Milk Lake hike exhausting at 4,600 m. Allow extra time, take frequent breaks, and descend immediately if symptoms worsen.
- **Weather changes instantly.** Sunny warmth can shift to cold winds and rain within minutes. Waterproof jacket required year-round.
- **Bring your own food and water** for hikes — only one small shop inside the park.
- **Horseback riding** available from Luorong Pasture toward the upper lakes, but expect to walk the steepest sections anyway.
- **Swimming prohibited** — lakes are sacred and glacier-fed (dangerously cold).
- **Minimal English spoken.** Phrasebook or translation app essential.
- **Maps:** 10 RMB at ticket office (Chinese only).
- **Pack layers:** Morning temp near freezing, midday can reach 20°C in sun, drops sharply after sunset.
- **Trekking poles** highly recommended for the Milk Lake/Five Colour Lake ascent.
- **Acclimatize in Daocheng (3,700 m) for 1 day** before entering the reserve if coming from low elevation.
