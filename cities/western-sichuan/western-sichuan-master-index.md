# Western Sichuan (川西) — Reference Index

## Articles & Guides

- **[fabio-nodari-western-sichuan-guide.md](fabio-nodari-western-sichuan-guide.md)** — "Exploring Western Sichuan: Travel Guide" by Fabio Nodari (fabionodariphoto.com, Jan 2026). Tibetan Plateau itinerary from Chengdu: Four Sisters Mountain, Danba villages, Longdeng Grassland, Tagong, Litang. Agency vs DIY advice, altitude preparation.

- **[travel-bunny-sichuan-qinghai-itinerary.md](travel-bunny-sichuan-qinghai-itinerary.md)** — "Two Weeks in Sichuan & Qinghai" by Suzanne Jones (thetravelbunny.com, Dec 2025). Week 1 road trip through Garze Prefecture: Wolong pandas, Zhonglu village, Tagong grassland (nomadic yak herders), Litang, Changquingchunkeer Monastery, Batang, Baiyu. No TAR permits needed.

- **[travel-bunny-tibetan-plateau-road-trip.md](travel-bunny-tibetan-plateau-road-trip.md)** — "Tibetan Plateau Road Trip: Sichuan to Qinghai" by Suzanne Jones (thetravelbunny.com, Feb 2026). Week 2: Baiyu → Dege (Dzogchen Monastery) → Dege Parkhang printing house → Yushu (Jiana Mani Stone City) → wildlife safari → Ta'er/Kumbum Monastery → Xining night market.

- **[chinadiscovery-jiuzhaigou-huanglong-guide.md](chinadiscovery-jiuzhaigou-huanglong-guide.md)** — "Jiuzhaigou & Huanglong Complete Guide" (chinadiscovery.com + others, 2025). 108 turquoise lakes, Nuo Ri Lang waterfall (320m wide). New 2024 train (1.5 hrs from Chengdu). Huanglong travertine terraces at 3,600m. Five-Color Pond, best late Sep–Oct.

- **[jennyfaraway-yading-nature-reserve-guide.md](jennyfaraway-yading-nature-reserve-guide.md)** — "Yading Nature Reserve Hiking Guide" (jennyfaraway.com). Three sacred mountains (Chenrezig 6,032m). Short loop to Pearl Lake (4,100m), long loop to Milk Lake (4,600m) and Five Colour Lake (4,700m). Entry 160 + 120 RMB bus. YHA dorm 25 RMB.

- **[asiaodyssey-sichuan-tibet-highway-guide.md](asiaodyssey-sichuan-tibet-highway-guide.md)** — "Sichuan-Tibet Highway G318 vs G317" (asiaodysseytravel.com, 2025). Southern G318 (2,142km, 7–10 days): Kangding, Xinduqiao, Litang, 72 Turns of Nujiang. Northern G317 (2,200km): Danba watchtowers, Dege Parkhang. Tibet permit requirements, road conditions.

- **[windhorsetour-sichuan-tibetan-monastery-culture-guide.md](windhorsetour-sichuan-tibetan-monastery-culture-guide.md)** — "Sichuan Tibetan Monasteries & Culture" (windhorsetour.com + chinadiscovery.com, 2025). Larung Gar (world's largest Buddhist academy), Yarchen Gar nunnery, Litang Monastery, Tagong, Dege Parkhang. Kham culture, Litang Horse Racing Festival (August), sky burial, no TAR permits.

- **[chinadiscovery-western-sichuan-altitude-logistics-guide.md](chinadiscovery-western-sichuan-altitude-logistics-guide.md)** — "Western Sichuan Altitude & Practical Tips" (composite sources, 2025). Elevation table (500m Chengdu to 4,700m Yading), Rhodiola Rosea protocol, road conditions, cash requirements (¥1,000+), packing essentials (oxygen tanks). Best mid-May through October.
