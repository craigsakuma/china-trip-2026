# Budget Breakdown: Backpacking Xinjiang (新疆)

**Source:** Alex Reynolds (lostwithpurpose.com), solo female traveler who lived in China for a year. Published budget data from 22 days backpacking across Xinjiang. First-hand costs.

---

## Quick Facts

- **Trip length:** 22 days
- **Total cost per person:** 3,369 RMB / €461 / $489
- **Daily average:** 153 RMB / €21 / ~$22/day
- **Travel style:** Budget backpacker — hostels, street food, public transport

## Food & Drinks — 50–80 RMB/day ($7–12)

Budget meals and street food for most meals. Uyghur food is cheap and filling — laghman (拉面, pulled noodles), polo (手抓饭, pilaf), samsa (烤包子, baked dumplings), kebabs.

---

## Accommodation — 50–70 RMB/day ($7–10)

Hostel dorm beds. Key foreigner-friendly options:

| City | Hostel | Price |
|------|--------|------:|
| Urumqi | Maitain Hostel | 45 RMB/dorm |
| Turpan | Dap Youth Hostel | 50 RMB/dorm |
| Kashgar | Old Town Hostel | 140 RMB/double |
| Karakul Lake | Camping | 87 RMB/day total |

**Note:** Many hotels refuse foreigners. The hostels above are confirmed to accept foreign passports.

---

## Transport Costs

| Method | Cost Range (RMB) |
|--------|----------------:|
| Long-distance bus | 100–200 |
| Hard-seat train | 100–200 |
| In-town taxi | 10–50 |
| Scenic area shuttle buses | 100–150 |

Distances in Xinjiang are enormous — budget significant transport costs. Buses and hard-seat trains are the cheapest intercity options.

---

## Attractions — 30–75 RMB/entry ($4–11)

Most historical sites and natural parks charge entrance fees in this range. Scenic shuttle buses within parks can add 100–150 RMB on top.

## Practical Tips

- **Walk or take public transport** — taxis add up fast across 22 days.
- **Eat twice daily** at cheap local restaurants — Uyghur street food keeps costs low.
- **Choose basic accommodation** — dorm beds at 45–50 RMB beat private rooms.
- **Karakul Lake camping** was the cheapest day of the trip at 87 RMB total.
- **Kashgar is the priciest stop** at ~230 RMB/day — double rooms and bazaar spending.
- **Scenic shuttle buses** are often mandatory and cost 100–150 RMB — budget for these at national parks.
- **Currency:** RMB (CNY) only. WeChat Pay / Alipay increasingly required; cash backup essential for rural areas.
- **Budget ceiling:** Even with occasional splurges, staying under $25/day is very achievable in Xinjiang.
