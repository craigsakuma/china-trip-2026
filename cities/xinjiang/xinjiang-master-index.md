# Xinjiang (新疆) — Reference Index

## Articles & Guides

- **[lost-with-purpose-xinjiang-best-places.md](lost-with-purpose-xinjiang-best-places.md)** — "12 Best Places to Visit in Xinjiang" by Alex Reynolds (lostwithpurpose.com). Urumqi, Turpan, Kashgar, Tashkurgan, Taklamakan, Khotan, Duku Highway, Sayram Lake, Bogda Shan, Hami, Kanas Lake, K2 base camp. Foreigner-friendly hostels, permits, ethical considerations.

- **[lost-with-purpose-xinjiang-budget.md](lost-with-purpose-xinjiang-budget.md)** — "Budget Breakdown: Backpacking Xinjiang" by Alex Reynolds (lostwithpurpose.com). 22-day budget at ~153 RMB/day ($22). City-by-city costs (Urumqi, Turpan, Kashgar, Karakul Lake), hostel prices, transport, money-saving strategies.

- **[nonstoptravelling-kashgar-silk-road-guide.md](nonstoptravelling-kashgar-silk-road-guide.md)** — "Kashgar Silk Road City Guide" (nonstoptravelling.com, 2024). Reconstructed Old Town, Id Kah Mosque (largest in China), Sunday Livestock Market, Afaq Khoja Mausoleum, Uyghur food, century-old teahouse, day trips to Karakul Lake and Tashkurgan.

- **[farwestchina-karakorum-highway-guide.md](farwestchina-karakorum-highway-guide.md)** — "Karakorum Highway China Section" by Josh Summers (farwestchina.com, 2024). Every stop Kashgar to Tashkurgan: Karakul Lake, Subash Pass (4,100m). Bus 51 RMB or driver 400–800 RMB. Permits, photography spots, accommodation.

- **[farwestchina-uyghur-food-guide.md](farwestchina-uyghur-food-guide.md)** — "Uyghur Food: Essential Xinjiang Cuisine" by Josh Summers (farwestchina.com). Top 10 dishes: Polo (pilaf), Laghman (hand-pulled noodles), lamb kebabs, nan, Samsa, DaPanJi (big plate chicken). Xinjiang fruits (Hami melon, Turpan grapes). Bazaar culture.

- **[windhorsetour-turpan-desert-guide.md](windhorsetour-turpan-desert-guide.md)** — "Turpan Silk Road Oasis & Desert Guide" (windhorsetour.com). Jiaohe Ancient City (UNESCO, 200 BC), Gaochang Ruins, Bezeklik Caves, Flaming Mountains, Grape Festival (Aug 18–28), Karez irrigation, Ayding Lake (-155m). Best Apr–Jun or Sep–Oct.

- **[farwestchina-xinjiang-safety-logistics-guide.md](farwestchina-xinjiang-safety-logistics-guide.md)** — "Xinjiang Safety & Logistics for Foreign Travelers" by Josh Summers (farwestchina.com, 2025). Safe verdict with zero tourist incidents. Security checkpoints, passport rules, Border Travel Permits, hotel registration, visa-free policies, VPN, time zone, cultural etiquette. 10+ years resident.
