# Yangtze River Three Gorges Cruise Guide — Chongqing to Yichang

**Source:** Yangtze River Cruises (yangtze-river-cruises.com/yangtze-river-guide/three-gorges-cruise.html) with supplementary detail from Travel China Guide and Travels with Talek. Comprehensive guide to the classic Yangtze cruise from Chongqing.

## Quick Facts

- **Route:** Chongqing → Yichang (downstream, 4 days/3 nights) or Yichang → Chongqing (upstream, 5 days/4 nights).
- **Distance:** ~660 km through the Three Gorges.
- **Departure dock:** Chaotianmen Dock (朝天门码头), Chongqing, at the confluence of the Yangtze and Jialing rivers.
- **Arrival dock:** Maoping Dock (茅坪码头), near Yichang.
- **Over 90% of cruisers** choose the downstream Chongqing→Yichang route as their first Yangtze experience.
- **Ship standard:** Most ships operate at 4-5 star luxury level with full-service dining, entertainment, and cabins with river-view balconies.
- **Best months:** April, May, September, October. November for red autumn foliage and lower prices.

## The Three Gorges (三峡)

### Qutang Gorge (瞿塘峡) — The Shortest & Most Dramatic
Length: 8 km. The narrowest gorge, with sheer cliff walls rising 1,200+ meters on both sides. The entrance, known as Kuimen (夔门), appears on the ¥10 banknote. Despite its short length, it's the most photographed section — towering vertical rock faces create a dramatic corridor effect. Ancient plank roads carved into the cliff faces are visible.

### Wu Gorge (巫峡) — The Most Beautiful
Length: 46 km. Known for its twelve peaks along both banks, perpetual mists, and lush vegetation. Goddess Peak (神女峰) is the most famous — a slender rock formation said to resemble a maiden gazing at the river. The gorge is winding, creating constantly shifting perspectives. Best for photography when morning mist lingers.

### Xiling Gorge (西陵峡) — The Longest
Length: 76 km. The most diverse gorge with rapids (now calmed by the dam), limestone caves, and varied terrain. Contains multiple sub-gorges including Shadow Play Gorge, Ox Liver and Horse Lung Gorge, and Kongling Gorge. The Three Gorges Dam sits at the eastern end.

## Shore Excursions

Most cruises include 3-4 shore excursions. The standard package typically covers:

### Three Gorges Dam (三峡大坝)
The world's largest hydroelectric dam, completed 2006. Generates 22,500 MW of power. The Three Gorges Dam Ship Lift is a modern engineering marvel — the world's largest ship elevator, lifting vessels 113 meters vertically in minutes. Some cruise itineraries include a transit through the ship lift (a memorable experience). Viewing platforms offer panoramic dam perspectives.

### Shennong Stream (神农溪) or Goddess Stream (神女溪)
Small tributary excursions by wooden sampan or peapod boat through narrow, mist-shrouded gorges. Shennong Stream features traditional Tujia boatmen who pole the boats upstream through shallow rapids. Goddess Stream is quieter and more intimate. Both offer close-up encounters with the gorge landscape impossible to see from the main river.

### Fengdu Ghost City (丰都鬼城)
A hilltop temple complex depicting the Chinese underworld and afterlife. Statues of demons, judges of the dead, and scenes of Buddhist/Taoist hell. Interesting rather than scary — combines folklore, religion, and dark humor. Involves a steep climb (cable car available). Some itineraries substitute Shibaozhai.

### Shibaozhai Pagoda (石宝寨)
A 12-story wooden pagoda built against a sheer cliff face beside the Yangtze. Originally accessible only by boat, now connected by a bridge after reservoir flooding. One of the most architecturally striking sites along the route. 200+ steps to the top with river views from each level.

### White Emperor City (白帝城)
Historic temple at the entrance to Qutang Gorge, associated with Three Kingdoms-era history. The poet Li Bai wrote his famous "Departing from Baidi City at Dawn" here. Now sits on an island after the dam raised water levels. Reached by bridge.

## Itinerary: 4-Day Downstream (Most Popular)

### Day 1 — Chongqing Embarkation
Board at Chaotianmen Dock between 17:00-20:00 (varies by cruise line). Ship departs late evening. Welcome dinner and safety briefing. Night sailing through the city — see Chongqing's illuminated skyline from the water.

### Day 2 — Fengdu / Shibaozhai
Morning shore excursion to Fengdu Ghost City or Shibaozhai Pagoda (varies by itinerary). Afternoon sailing through the reservoir section. Evening entertainment onboard.

### Day 3 — The Three Gorges
The highlight day. Transit through Qutang Gorge (early morning — set your alarm) and Wu Gorge. Mid-morning: side-trip to Shennong Stream or Goddess Stream by small boat. Afternoon: Enter Xiling Gorge. Evening: Ship approaches the dam.

### Day 4 — Three Gorges Dam & Disembarkation
Morning visit to the Three Gorges Dam viewing area. Some ships transit the ship lift (check itinerary). Disembark at Maoping Dock by noon. Transfer to Yichang city center or Yichang East Train Station for onward travel.

## Booking & Logistics

### When to Book
Book 2-3 months ahead for peak season (April-May, September-October). November offers good availability and lower prices. Chinese holiday weeks (Golden Week, May Day) sell out months in advance.

### Choosing a Cabin
- **Standard cabin (no balcony):** Cheapest option. River views through a window.
- **Balcony cabin:** Worth the upgrade — watching the gorges from your private balcony is the signature experience.
- **Executive/suite:** Larger rooms, better views, sometimes include butler service.
- **Deck choice:** Higher decks have better views but more engine vibration on some ships. Mid-decks are often the smoothest ride.

### What to Pack
- Layers (mornings on deck are cool, especially in October)
- Rain jacket or umbrella
- Binoculars (for spotting wildlife and cliff details)
- Camera with zoom lens
- Comfortable walking shoes (for shore excursions with significant climbing)
- Formal-ish outfit for Captain's dinner (smart casual is fine)
- Motion sickness remedies (river is generally calm but some sections have mild movement)
- Cash for tips and onboard purchases

### Connecting to Chongqing Sightseeing
Most travelers spend 2-3 days in Chongqing before the cruise. Chaotianmen Dock is centrally located in Yuzhong District, walking distance from Jiefangbei and Hongya Cave. Plan your Chongqing city exploration before boarding.

## Practical Tips

- **October timing (our trip):** Excellent month for the cruise. Comfortable temperatures (15-22°C), post-Golden Week crowds, early autumn foliage beginning in the gorges.
- **Sunrise in Qutang Gorge** is spectacular. The ship typically passes through in early morning of Day 3. Wake up early and be on deck by 06:30.
- **River water color:** The Yangtze is brown/muddy (sediment-rich). This is normal. The confluence with the clearer Jialing River in Chongqing creates a visible two-tone meeting line.
- **Dam water level:** The reservoir's water level fluctuates seasonally, affecting gorge scenery. Autumn levels are typically moderate, preserving good gorge wall visibility.
- **Phone signal:** Spotty in the gorges. Download entertainment, books, and offline maps before boarding.
