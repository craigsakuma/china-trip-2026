# Leshan Practical Logistics: Transport from Chengdu & Getting Around

**Source:** ChinaDiscovery (chinadiscovery.com), comprehensive Leshan transportation guide. Supplemented with data from AsiaOdysseyTravel and TopChinaTravel.

## Quick Facts

- **Distance from Chengdu:** ~140 km (Leshan Giant Buddha scenic area: 126 km)
- **Best transport:** High-speed train (~1 hour, 54 RMB)
- **Trains per day:** 30+ bullet trains Chengdu-Leshan
- **Day trip viable:** Yes — depart 7-8 AM, return by 5-6 PM
- **Leshan to Emeishan:** 15-minute train (one station apart)
- **Leshan Station to Giant Buddha:** 13 km; 30 min by taxi, 1 hour by bus

---

## High-Speed Train: Chengdu to Leshan

### Departure Stations in Chengdu

#### Chengdu East Station (成都东站)
- **Location:** East Chengdu, ~7 km from city center
- **Metro access:** Lines 2 and 7
- **Most trains:** Majority of Leshan services depart here
- **Journey time:** 46 minutes to 1 hour 16 minutes

#### Chengdu South Station (成都南站)
- **Location:** High-tech zone, ~13 km southwest of center
- **Metro access:** Lines 1 and 7
- **Journey time:** Similar to East Station

#### Shuangliu Airport Station (双流机场站)
- **Location:** Basement 2, Terminal 2 of Chengdu Shuangliu International Airport
- **Direct airport-to-Leshan:** 10+ C-trains daily
- **Journey time:** 35-50 minutes
- **Fare:** 46 RMB (2nd class) / 73 RMB (1st class)
- **Ideal for:** International arrivals connecting directly to Leshan

### Arrival: Leshan Station (乐山站)
- Located ~13 km from the Giant Buddha scenic area
- **Taxi:** ~15 minutes to Giant Buddha; ~30 RMB
- **Tourist bus:** ~30 minutes to Giant Buddha
- **Local bus 605 or Bus 3:** ~1 hour to Giant Buddha (cheapest option, ~2 RMB)

## Bus: Chengdu to Leshan

### Primary: Xinnanmen Bus Station (新南门汽车站)
- **Address:** No. 2 Xinnan Road, Wuhou District
- **Metro:** Line 3 (Xinnanmen station)
- **Frequency:** Every 20 minutes
- **Duration:** ~2 hours
- **Arrives at:** Leshan Xiaoba Tourism Station (乐山肖坝旅游车站)

### From Xiaoba Station to Giant Buddha
- **Local Bus 13:** Direct to Giant Buddha, ~1 hour
- **Taxi:** ~15 minutes (5 km), ~15-20 RMB

### Other Chengdu Bus Stations
- Chengdu North Bus Station, Shiyangchang Bus Station, and East Bus Station also serve Leshan
- These options require transfers upon arrival — less convenient than Xinnanmen

## Leshan to Emeishan (Mount Emei)

### High-Speed Train
- **Distance:** One station apart on the rail line
- **Duration:** ~15 minutes
- **Frequency:** ~10 daily services
- **Fare:** 11 RMB (2nd class) / 17 RMB (1st class)
- **Road distance:** ~50 km

### Bus
- Regular services from Leshan bus station
- Duration: ~1 hour
- Less convenient than train

## Visiting the Giant Buddha — Logistics

### Tickets
- **Scenic area (walking):** 80 RMB
- **Boat cruise:** 70 RMB (separate ticket; purchased at river pier)
- **Combined options:** Some packages available

### Opening Hours
- **April-October:** 7:30 AM - 6:30 PM
- **November-March:** 8:00 AM - 5:30 PM
- **Boat cruises:** 7:30 AM - 6:00 PM

### Time Allocation
- **Walking route only:** 3-4 hours (including queues)
- **Boat cruise only:** 1 hour (including waiting)
- **Both routes:** 4-5 hours
- **With Lingyun + Wuyou Temples:** Full day

### Queue Management
- **Best arrival time:** Before 8:30 AM — dramatically shorter queues
- **Best days:** Tuesday-Thursday
- **Worst times:** National holidays (Oct 1-7, May 1-5), weekends, summer holidays
- "Set aside the best part of a day (about 10 AM to 3 PM)" for a thorough visit

## Booking Tips

- **Train tickets:** Book via 12306.cn (official), Trip.com, or China Highlights
- **Advance booking:** 2-3 days ahead during peak season; same-day usually fine off-peak
- **Payment:** International credit cards accepted on Trip.com; 12306 requires Chinese payment
- **Passport:** Required for ticket purchase and boarding; carry original
- **Station arrival:** Arrive 30-40 minutes before departure for security screening

---

## Practical Tips

- The 06:13 G2195 is the earliest train — perfect for beating crowds at the Buddha
- From Shuangliu Airport, direct trains to Leshan mean you can visit the same day you land
- Leshan Station has luggage storage if you're transiting to Emeishan
- Bring your own water and snacks; options inside the scenic area are limited and expensive
- Download Didi (Chinese Uber) for easy taxi booking at Leshan Station
- 144-hour visa-free transit through Chengdu covers Leshan visits for qualifying nationalities
- Return trains to Chengdu run until approximately 9 PM — no risk of being stranded
- If combining Leshan + Emeishan, the 15-minute inter-city train makes logistics seamless
