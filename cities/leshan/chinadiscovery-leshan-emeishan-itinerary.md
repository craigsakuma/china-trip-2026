# Leshan + Emeishan Combined Itinerary: 2-3 Day UNESCO Circuit

**Source:** ChinaDiscovery (chinadiscovery.com) and SichuanTravelGuide. Comprehensive planning guide for the Leshan-Emeishan UNESCO twin sites.

## Quick Facts

- **Two UNESCO World Heritage Sites** listed jointly since 1996
- **Distance apart:** ~50 km (30 minutes by car, 15 minutes by train)
- **From Chengdu:** ~140 km to Leshan; ~160 km to Mount Emei
- **Duration:** 2 days minimum; 3-4 days ideal for hiking Mount Emei
- **Best time:** April-May, September-October (mild weather, fewer crowds)
- **Theme:** Buddhism — Giant Buddha for protection; Mount Emei as sacred pilgrimage

---

## Recommended 2-Day Itinerary

### Day 1: Chengdu to Leshan Giant Buddha

**Morning**
- Depart Chengdu ~7:30 AM
- High-speed train from Chengdu East (成都东站) to Leshan Station (乐山站)
- Journey: 46 minutes to 1 hour 16 minutes
- Fare: 54 RMB (2nd class) / 86 RMB (1st class)
- From Leshan Station: bus 605/3 (~1 hour) or taxi (~15 minutes, ~30 RMB)

**At the Giant Buddha**
- **Option A (immersive):** Walk the Nine Bends Plank Road down to Buddha's feet
  - Queue + descent: 2-3 hours; return climb: ~1 hour
  - Include Lingyun Temple and Wuyou Temple on the mountain
  - Full scenic area: 80 RMB entry
- **Option B (efficient):** River boat cruise for panoramic view
  - 40-minute cruise: 70 RMB
  - Best photography angle; avoids plank road queues
- **Ideal:** Do both if time allows (boat first for overview, then walk)

**Afternoon / Evening**
- Lunch at Zhanggongqiao food street (张公桥) — try Qiaojiao Beef and Boboji
- Transfer to Mount Emei area: High-speed train 15 minutes (11-17 RMB) or 1-hour drive
- Check into hotel near Baoguo Temple (报国寺) at the mountain base
- Evening: Explore Baoguo village; rest for tomorrow's hike

### Day 2: Mount Emei — Golden Summit

**If Hiking (2-day mountain option)**
- Start early from Baoguo Temple
- Take sightseeing shuttle bus to Leidongping Parking Lot (雷洞坪): 90 RMB round trip
- Walk ~30 minutes to Jieyin Temple (接引殿)
- **Option A:** Hike 2.5 hours to Golden Summit (3,079m)
- **Option B:** Cable car to summit (65 RMB up, 55 RMB down, ~20 minutes)
- At Golden Summit: View the 48-meter golden Samantabhadra statue, sea of clouds, panoramic views
- Descend by same route or alternative trail

**If Full Hiking (3-day option)**
- Day 2: Hike from Baoguo through forest trails to mid-mountain temple accommodation
- Day 3: Summit push to Golden Summit; descend by bus/cable car
- More immersive; includes monkey encounters, waterfalls, temple stays

**Return to Chengdu**
- From Emeishan Station: High-speed train ~1 hour back to Chengdu
- Fare: ~110 RMB (2nd class)
- Last trains typically depart by 9 PM

## Extended Options (3-5 Days)

### 3 Days: Leshan + Full Emei Hike
- Day 1: Leshan Giant Buddha (full day)
- Day 2: Hike Emeishan base to mid-mountain temple
- Day 3: Summit + descent; return to Chengdu

### 4-5 Days: Complete Circuit
- Day 1: Leshan Giant Buddha + Leshan food exploration
- Day 2: Jiayang Steam Train (嘉阳小火车) through flower fields
- Day 3-4: Full Mount Emei hike with temple stays
- Day 5: Return to Chengdu

### 7+ Days: Regional Extension
- Add Jiuzhaigou Valley (九寨沟), Dazu Rock Carvings (大足石刻), or Dujiangyan (都江堰)

## Accommodation Options

### Leshan
- **Downtown:** Panlong Kaiyuan Mingdu Hotel (5-star); Orange Hotel Select (4-star)
- **Near Buddha:** Sangjaingyue Holiday Hotel; A Yard of Chan (禅意民宿)
- **Recommendation:** Stay in Leshan if doing food exploration; otherwise transit to Emei

### Mount Emei Base (Baoguo Village)
- Budget guesthouses and mid-range hotels clustered near Baoguo Temple
- Best base for starting early morning hikes

### Mount Emei Mountain
- Temple dormitories: 40-60 RMB/person
- Basic but atmospheric; the quintessential Emeishan experience
- Book in advance during peak seasons

### Alternative: Stay in Chengdu
- Greater amenities and restaurant variety
- Easy same-day return via high-speed train
- Best if you prefer city comfort over proximity

## Visa Information

- Citizens of 53 countries qualify for **144-hour visa-free transit** through Chengdu
- This covers Leshan and Emeishan visits during international layovers
- Requires: international transit ticket, valid passport, proper documentation
- Process at Chengdu Shuangliu or Tianfu airport immigration

## Practical Tips

- "A smart way" to see both UNESCO sites is combining them in one trip — they're only 30 minutes apart
- Leshan first, Emeishan second is the classic order (ascending difficulty)
- The Jiayang Steam Train (March for rapeseed flowers; Aug-Sept for sunflowers) is a worthwhile addition
- Pack layers: Leshan is warm lowland; Emeishan summit can be near freezing
- Bring rain gear regardless of season — mountain weather changes rapidly
- Book train tickets 2-3 days in advance during peak seasons via 12306.cn or Trip.com
- Most travelers find 2 days sufficient; hikers and food enthusiasts should plan 3-4
- The Giant Buddha and Golden Summit are both "must-see" experiences — do not skip either
