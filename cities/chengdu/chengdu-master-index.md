# Chengdu (成都) — Reference Index

## Articles & Guides

- **[fabio-nodari-chengdu-things-to-do.md](fabio-nodari-chengdu-things-to-do.md)** — "Top Things to Do in Chengdu" by Fabio Nodari (fabionodariphoto.com, Feb 2026). Pandas (Chengdu Center vs Wolong), Anshun Bridge, Kuanzhai Alley, temples (Wuhou, Daci), Jinsha Museum, Sichuan opera, hot pot, day trips to Leshan, Dujiangyan/Qingcheng, and Shangli.

- **[goats-on-the-road-chengdu-panda.md](goats-on-the-road-chengdu-panda.md)** — "Chengdu: Giant Panda Breeding Research Base" by Dariece Swift (goatsontheroad.com, Jul 2012). First-hand panda base visit by local bus, Wenshu Temple, Sichuan hot pot, tea house culture, budget accommodation at Sim's Cozy Guesthouse.

- **[chinadiscovery-chengdu-sichuan-food-guide.md](chinadiscovery-chengdu-sichuan-food-guide.md)** — "Chengdu Sichuan Food Guide" (chinadiscovery.com + The Food Ranger). UNESCO City of Gastronomy: hotpot (XiaoLongKan, maocai, chuanchuan), signature dishes (mapo tofu, kung pao chicken, fuqi feipian), noodles, snacks (rabbit head), food neighborhoods (Yulin Road, Jinli, Jianshe Road Night Market). Prices and spice-level ordering phrases.

- **[chinadiscovery-chengdu-teahouse-nightlife.md](chinadiscovery-chengdu-teahouse-nightlife.md)** — "Chengdu Teahouse Culture & Nightlife" (chinadiscovery.com). 10,000+ teahouses (Heming in People's Park), gaiwan customs, ear cleaning, mahjong. Sichuan opera at ShuFengYaYun (¥140–320, face-changing). Night markets (Jinli, Kuanzhai, Jianshe Road). Bar streets (Jiuyanqiao, LanGuiFang). Chunxi Road IFS panda.

- **[chinasurvivalkit-chengdu-panda-base-guide.md](chinasurvivalkit-chengdu-panda-base-guide.md)** — "Chengdu Panda Base Complete Visitor Guide" (chinasurvivalkit.com, 2025). Tickets ¥55, hours 07:30–18:00, best 08:00–10:00 for feeding. West Gate recommended. 200+ pandas, nursery, museum. Metro Line 3 + D025 bus or taxi ¥50–80. Baby panda season Jul–Sep. 3–4 hour visit.

- **[sichuantravelguide-chengdu-day-trips.md](sichuantravelguide-chengdu-day-trips.md)** — "Chengdu Day Trips: Leshan, Emeishan & Dujiangyan" (sichuantravelguide.com). Leshan Giant Buddha (71m, ¥80, 45 min train). Mount Emei (Golden Summit 3,077m, ¥160 + shuttle ¥90). Dujiangyan irrigation (¥80, 30 min train). Combined itineraries and Qingcheng Mountain.

- **[chengdu-expat-first-timer-practical-guide.md](chengdu-expat-first-timer-practical-guide.md)** — "Chengdu First-Timer Practical Guide" (chengdu-expat.com). Airports (Tianfu TFU, Shuangliu CTU), metro, Didi, Alipay/WeChat setup, neighborhoods (Chunxi Road, Kuanzhai, Anshun Bridge), October weather 15–22°C, 240-hour visa-free transit, packing list, Mandarin phrases.
