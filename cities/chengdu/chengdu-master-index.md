# Chengdu (成都) — Reference Index

## Articles & Guides

- **[fabio-nodari-chengdu-things-to-do.md](fabio-nodari-chengdu-things-to-do.md)** — "Top Things to Do in Chengdu" by Fabio Nodari (fabionodariphoto.com, Feb 2026). Comprehensive guide covering pandas (Chengdu Center vs Wolong), Anshun Bridge, Kuanzhai Alley, temples (Wuhou Shrine, Daci Temple), Jinsha Museum, Sichuan opera, hot pot, and day trips to Leshan Giant Buddha, Dujiangyan/Qingcheng Mountain, and Shangli Old Town.

- **[goats-on-the-road-chengdu-panda.md](goats-on-the-road-chengdu-panda.md)** — "Chengdu: Giant Panda Breeding Research Base & Budget Backpacker Guide" by Dariece Swift (goatsontheroad.com, Jul 2012). First-hand account of visiting the Panda Base independently by local bus, plus Wenshu Temple, Sichuan hot pot, tea house culture, and budget accommodation at Sim's Cozy Guesthouse. Includes train travel tips (hard seater vs hard sleeper classes) and language barrier strategies.
