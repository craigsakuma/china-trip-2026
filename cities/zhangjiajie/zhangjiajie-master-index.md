Includes accommodation picks, budget breakdown (~221 USD/3 days), transport logistics, and seasonal advice.

- **[bucketlistly-tianmen-mountain-guide.md](bucketlistly-tianmen-mountain-guide.md)** — "Visiting Tianmen Mountain: A Complete Travel Guide" by Pete R. (bucketlistly.blog, Feb 2026). Deep-dive single-attraction guide: Route A/B/C comparison (Route B strongly recommended), Middle Station sea-of-clouds views, Gateway to Heaven/Tianmen Cave (131.5m arch, 999 steps, 32 CNY escalator), 99 Bends Road, three Glass Skywalks (Coiling Dragon Cliff longest at 100m), Yunmeng Fairy Peak (1,518.6m summit), Forest Sightseeing Cable Car (25 CNY), Tianmen Temple. Tickets 288 CNY, 5–7 hours needed.
