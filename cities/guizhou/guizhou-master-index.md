# Guizhou (贵州) — Reference Index

## Articles & Guides

- **[fabio-nodari-zhaoxing-dong-guide.md](fabio-nodari-zhaoxing-dong-guide.md)** — "Zhaoxing Dong Village: Quick Travel Guide" by Fabio Nodari (fabionodariphoto.com, Jan 2026). Dong minority culture, Drum Towers and Wind-Rain Bridges (built without nails), embroidery/batik, Tangan village day trip, sour soup fish, bamboo rice.

- **[lost-with-purpose-guizhou-villages.md](lost-with-purpose-guizhou-villages.md)** — "Off-the-Beaten-Track Villages in Guizhou" by Alex Reynolds (lostwithpurpose.com). Day trips from Kaili: Matang (wax batik), Langde (stilted houses, rice paddies), Zhouxi (indigo dyeing, buffalo fighting). Transport, homestay contacts, off-season timing.

- **[chinahighlights-huangguoshu-waterfall-guide.md](chinahighlights-huangguoshu-waterfall-guide.md)** — "Huangguoshu Waterfall Complete Guide" (chinahighlights.com). Asia's largest waterfall (77.8m, 101m wide). Three scenic sections: Doupotang, Grand Waterfall with Water Curtain Cave, Tianxingqiao. 230 RMB admission. Best May–Jun or Sep–Oct.

- **[chinadiscovery-xijiang-miao-village-guide.md](chinadiscovery-xijiang-miao-village-guide.md)** — "Xijiang Qianhu Miao Village" (chinadiscovery.com). China's largest Miao settlement (1,432 households). Stilted houses, silver jewelry, Lusheng dancing, Long Table Banquet, Guzang/Sisters' Meal festivals. 100 RMB, 32km from Kaili.

- **[chinahighlights-zhenyuan-ancient-town-guide.md](chinahighlights-zhenyuan-ancient-town-guide.md)** — "Zhenyuan Ancient Town" (chinahighlights.com). 2,200-year-old S-shaped river town. Qinglongdong cliff complex, Ming/Qing alleys, evening river cruises. Midpoint between Fanjingshan and Kaili. Tofu-fish and sour beef hotpot.

- **[chinahighlights-guizhou-food-guide.md](chinahighlights-guizhou-food-guide.md)** — "Guizhou Cuisine Guide" (chinahighlights.com). Sour-and-spicy philosophy. Sour soup fish (red vs white broth), Changwang noodles, Siwawa, Qingyan braised pork hock. Night markets (Qingyun Road, Erqi Road). Fish mint (折耳根), 40+ noodle varieties.

- **[recyourtrip-guizhou-7day-itinerary.md](recyourtrip-guizhou-7day-itinerary.md)** — "Guizhou 7-Day Itinerary" (recyourtrip.com). Guiyang → Huangguoshu → Wanfenglin → Zhijin Cave → Zhaoxing → Basha → Langde → Qingyan. Real distances, drive times, admission costs (750 RMB total), HSR + driving logistics.
