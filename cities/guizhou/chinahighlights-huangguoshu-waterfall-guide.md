# Huangguoshu Waterfall (黄果树瀑布) — Complete Visiting Guide

**Source:** China Highlights (chinahighlights.com), major China travel agency. Comprehensive guide covering the full scenic area including Doupotang, main waterfall, and Tianxingqiao.

## Quick Facts

- **Location:** Anshun (安顺), Guizhou Province, ~130 km from Guiyang (~2 hours by car).
- **Dimensions:** 77.8-meter drop, 101-meter width — Asia's largest waterfall.
- **Scenic area:** Three sections — Doupotang Waterfall, Grand Waterfall (main), Tianxing Bridge (Tianxingqiao).
- **UNESCO:** Part of the Huangguoshu Scenic Area, a national AAAAA attraction.
- **Best time:** May–June or September–October (strong water flow, fewer crowds). Avoid July–August (summer vacation, 1–2 hour elevator waits).

## Three Sections of the Scenic Area

### Doupotang Waterfall (陡坡塘瀑布)

Filming location for the TV series *Journey to the West*. Gentle, flat walking trails with an elevated perspective looking toward the grand waterfall. Allow ~40 minutes.

### Grand Waterfall — Huangguoshu Main Falls (黄果树大瀑布)

The centerpiece. Walking down takes ~30 minutes; escalators available both ways. Behind the falls sits the **Water Curtain Cave (水帘洞)** — a 134-meter-long natural cave passage that lets you walk behind the cascading water. Requires advance QR code reservation via the WeChat mini-program. Allow ~2 hours for this section.

### Tianxing Bridge Scenic Area (天星桥景区)

Features a funnel-shaped waterfall and karst cave exploration. The recommended lower section takes ~1 hour with stairs and flat sections. Battery carts and cable cars available for the return trip. Full exploration: 1–3 hours.

## Tickets & Reservations

- **Real-name timed entry system** — must reserve one day in advance via WeChat mini-program "One-Code Travel Guizhou" (一码游贵州).
- **Admission:** 230 RMB (waterfall + all three scenic areas, per Recyourtrip 2024 data).
- **Night tour available:** 8:00 PM and 9:00 PM slots, one hour duration.

## Seasonal Weather

- Spring: 10–25°C
- Summer: 20–30°C
- Autumn: 18–25°C
- Winter: 5–13°C

## Getting There from Guiyang (贵阳)

- **By car/taxi:** ~2 hours, 130 km via expressway.
- **By high-speed train:** Guiyang → Anshun (~30 minutes), then taxi/shuttle (~1 hour) to the scenic area.
- **Tour bus:** Available from Guiyang tourist bus stations.

## Recommended Visit Strategy

Stay overnight near Huangguoshu to enter before 9:00 AM and explore all three sections without rushing. Recommended order: Doupotang first → Grand Waterfall → Tianxingqiao last. Full day needed for unhurried exploration.

## Nearby Attractions Worth Combining

- **Balinghe Bridge (坝陵河大桥):** 10 km away, features a glass-bottom walkway over a 370-meter gorge.
- **Dragon Palace (龙宫):** 30 km away, China's largest water cave system with underground boat rides.
- **Tianlong Tunbao (天龙屯堡):** 40 km away, a 600-year-old Ming Dynasty military settlement where residents still wear traditional Han-era clothing.

## Accommodation

- **Huangguoshu Yingbin Hotel (黄果树迎宾馆):** Five-star, 4-minute drive from entrance.
- **Jianglu Village Dawn Resort:** 14 km from scenic area (30-minute drive), converted from traditional Buyi stone houses.

## Practical Tips

- Bring a raincoat or umbrella — you will get wet near the main falls and especially in Water Curtain Cave.
- Wear shoes with good traction — paths are wet, narrow, and uneven.
- Use the sightseeing shuttle bus between sections to save energy.
- November–April is low season with minimal crowds but weaker water flow.
- Combine with Zhijin Cave (织金洞) and Anshun old town for a 2–3 day Anshun itinerary.
