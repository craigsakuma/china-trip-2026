# Guizhou 7-Day Itinerary — Practical Road Trip Guide

**Source:** Recyourtrip (recyourtrip.com), independent travel blog with maps and logistics. First-hand 7-day driving itinerary covering waterfalls, caves, Dong and Miao villages, with real distances, drive times, and admission costs.

## Quick Facts

- **Duration:** 7 days covering ~1,000+ km through Guizhou.
- **Route:** Guiyang → Anshun/Huangguoshu → Xingyi/Wanfenglin → Zhijin Cave → Guiyang → Congjiang/Zhaoxing → Basha/Kaili → Langde → Guiyang → Qingyan.
- **Transport:** Mix of private car/van and high-speed rail.
- **Best time:** Spring (Apr–Jun) or autumn (Sep–Nov) for mild weather and festivals.
- **Key warning:** Distances appear shorter on maps but require significantly more drive time than expected on mountain roads.

## Day-by-Day Breakdown

### Day 1: Guiyang (贵阳) → Huangguoshu Area

- **Distance:** 164 km, ~2 hours 11 minutes.
- **Activity:** Travel day; settle near Anshun (安顺).
- **Hotel:** Huangguoshu Yingbinguan Hotel (黄果树迎宾馆).

### Day 2: Huangguoshu Waterfall (黄果树瀑布) + Wanfenglin (万峰林)

- **Morning:** Huangguoshu Waterfall visit (~2 hours). Admission: 230 RMB.
- **Afternoon:** Transfer 199 km (~3 hours) to Wanfenglin Scenic Area (Ten Thousand Peaks Forest). Admission: 110 RMB.
- **Evening:** Jilongbao Resort on lake shores, 21 km further (~30 minutes).

### Day 3: Zhijin Cave (织金洞) → Back to Guiyang

- **Main attraction:** Zhijin Cave — one of China's most spectacular cave systems.
- **Distance from resort:** 371 km, ~5 hours 25 minutes.
- **Admission:** 70 RMB. Cave closes at 4:00 PM — plan accordingly.
- **Evening:** Arrive in Guiyang.

### Day 4: Train to Zhaoxing Dong Village (肇兴侗寨)

- **Transport:** High-speed train Guiyang → Congjiang (从江), ~2 hours.
- **Onward:** Car to Zhaoxing village, 46 km, ~1 hour 10 minutes.
- **Admission:** 120 RMB.
- **Overnight:** Stay in the wooden Dong ethnic settlement. Recommended: Zhaoxing Indigo Hotel.
- **Side trip:** Tang'ancun village (堂安村) worth a 20-minute detour (8 km) — much quieter alternative.

### Day 5: Basha Miao Village (岜沙苗寨) → Kaili (凯里)

- **Morning:** Basha Miao Village — the "last gunmen tribe" where men still carry hunting rifles. Admission: 80 RMB.
- **Total distance:** 273 km to Kaili, ~5 hours 15 minutes.
- **Evening:** Arrive Kaili region.

### Day 6: Langde Miao Village (朗德苗寨) → Guiyang

- **Morning:** Langde Miao Village — stilted houses, rice paddies, welcome ceremony with rice wine at 11:00 AM. Admission: 70 RMB. Open until 9:00 PM.
- **Afternoon:** Drive back to Guiyang, 240 km, ~3 hours 40 minutes.

### Day 7: Qingyan Ancient Town (青岩古镇) → Departure

- **Distance:** 56 km from Guiyang, ~1 hour.
- **Activity:** City walls, museums, food stalls (famous braised pork hock). Admission: 70 RMB. Open until 9:00 PM.
- **Depart:** Return to Guiyang for flight/train out.

## Practical Tips

- Most sites operate 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM. Langde and Qingyan extend to 9:00 PM.
- By 6:00 PM it gets dark in November — summer travel offers extended daylight.
- Expect frequent schedule adjustments due to weather, delays, and early closures.
- Booking through a travel agency simplifies coordination between driving and train segments.
- Village performances at noon in Langde; evening ambiance in Zhaoxing.
- Karaoke is popular in Guiyang — locals consider it a social activity.
- Guizhou roads are winding mountain roads — motion sickness medication recommended.
- Download offline maps before entering remote areas — phone service can be spotty in mountains.
- Book Booking.com or Trip.com for accommodation; Trip.com is often cheaper for Chinese hotels.
