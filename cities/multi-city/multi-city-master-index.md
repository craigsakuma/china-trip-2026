# Multi-City China Travel — Reference Index

## Articles & Guides

- **[adventures-around-asia-off-beaten-path.md](adventures-around-asia-off-beaten-path.md)** — "10 Off-the-Beaten-Path Places to Visit in China" by Richelle (adventuresaroundasia.com). Covers Xi'an, Chongqing, Sichuan, Zhangjiajie, Harbin, Macau, Guilin/Yangshuo, Yunnan, Xinjiang, and Tibet. Duration estimates, transport tips, and first-hand opinions for each region.

- **[adventures-around-asia-china-bucket-list.md](adventures-around-asia-china-bucket-list.md)** — "50 Things for Your China Bucket List" by Richelle (adventuresaroundasia.com). All 50 items with locations, descriptions, prices, and personal recommendations. Spans Great Wall, Xi'an, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Harbin, Chengdu, Guilin, Tibet, Dunhuang, Zhangjiajie, Yunnan, Xinjiang, and more. Author completed 40 of 50.

- **[stoked-to-travel-two-week-china-itinerary.md](stoked-to-travel-two-week-china-itinerary.md)** — "The Perfect Two Week China Itinerary" by Claire (stokedtotravel.com). Day-by-day 14-day route: Beijing → Great Wall → Suzhou → Shanghai → Zhangjiajie → Tianmen Mountain → Yangshuo → Chengdu. Covers transport (flights, high-speed rail, maglev, subway), accommodation with prices, dining, visa/VPN/language tips. Independent travel perspective; author calls China "the most challenging place I have travelled around."

- **[a-way-abroad-unique-places-china.md](a-way-abroad-unique-places-china.md)** — "Most Unique Places to Go in China" by Catherine Tour (findawayabroad.com). Five off-the-beaten-path destinations: Liangzhu ruins (Hangzhou), Guanyin of Nanshan (Sanya), Feng Xiaogang Movie Town (Haikou), Thousand Islands Lake (Zhejiang), and Xixing Old Town (Hangzhou). Practical tips on local apps (Dianping, XiaoHongShu, Gaode Maps), exploring by bus/bike, and learning Chinese. Author lived in China six years.

- **[bucketlistly-south-southwest-china-itinerary.md](bucketlistly-south-southwest-china-itinerary.md)** — "One-Month Itinerary for South & Southwest China" by Pete R. (bucketlistly.blog). 34-day nature-focused route: Macau > Hong Kong > Guangzhou > Guilin > Yangshuo > Xingping > Guiyang > Huangguoshu Waterfall > Xijiang > Dali > Lijiang > Shangri-La > Yading > Chengdu > Leshan > Jiuzhaigou > Huanglong > Fenghuang > Zhangjiajie. Day-by-day with transport (trains, buses, flights, costs), accommodation at three price tiers per city, detailed attraction guides, and practical tips (apps, budget breakdown ~$80/day). Author traveled October–November; Yading is his favorite stop.
