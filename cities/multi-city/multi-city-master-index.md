# Multi-City China Travel — Reference Index

## Articles & Guides

- **[adventures-around-asia-off-beaten-path.md](adventures-around-asia-off-beaten-path.md)** — "10 Off-the-Beaten-Path Places in China" by Richelle (adventuresaroundasia.com). Xi'an, Chongqing, Sichuan, Zhangjiajie, Harbin, Macau, Guilin/Yangshuo, Yunnan, Xinjiang, Tibet. Duration estimates and transport tips for each.

- **[adventures-around-asia-china-bucket-list.md](adventures-around-asia-china-bucket-list.md)** — "50 Things for Your China Bucket List" by Richelle (adventuresaroundasia.com). All 50 items with locations, prices, first-hand opinions. Great Wall (5 sections ranked), Xi'an food, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Harbin, Chengdu, Guilin, Tibet, Dunhuang, Zhangjiajie.

- **[stoked-to-travel-two-week-china-itinerary.md](stoked-to-travel-two-week-china-itinerary.md)** — "The Perfect Two Week China Itinerary" by Claire (stokedtotravel.com). Beijing → Great Wall → Suzhou → Shanghai → Zhangjiajie → Tianmen → Yangshuo → Chengdu. Transport, accommodation with prices, visa/VPN/language tips.

- **[a-way-abroad-unique-places-china.md](a-way-abroad-unique-places-china.md)** — "Most Unique Places to Go in China" by Catherine Tour (findawayabroad.com). Five hidden gems: Liangzhu ruins (Hangzhou), Guanyin of Nanshan (Sanya), Movie Town (Haikou), Thousand Islands Lake (Zhejiang), Xixing Old Town. App tips (Dianping, XiaoHongShu).

- **[bucketlistly-south-southwest-china-itinerary.md](bucketlistly-south-southwest-china-itinerary.md)** — "One-Month South & Southwest China Itinerary" by Pete R. (bucketlistly.blog). 34-day route: Macau → Hong Kong → Guangzhou → Guilin → Yangshuo → Guiyang → Dali → Lijiang → Shangri-La → Yading → Chengdu → Leshan → Jiuzhaigou → Fenghuang → Zhangjiajie. ~$80/day budget, Oct–Nov timing.
