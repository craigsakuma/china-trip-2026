# Guangzhou (广州) — Reference Index

## Articles & Guides

- **[bucketlistly-guangzhou-things-to-do.md](bucketlistly-guangzhou-things-to-do.md)** — "Explore Guangzhou: 12 Incredible Things to Do" by Pete R. (bucketlistly.blog). Canton Tower, Chen Clan Ancestral Hall, Shamian Island, Yuexiu Park, Moxing Mountain, Beijing Road, Sacred Heart Cathedral, Shangxiajiu. Metro/transport tips, district-by-district planning.

- **[eatingadventures-dim-sum-guide.md](eatingadventures-dim-sum-guide.md)** — "Best Dim Sum in Guangzhou" (eatingadventures.com). 13 restaurants: Bei Yuan, Nan Yuan, Guangzhou Restaurant (est. 1935), Dian Dou De (108 varieties), Jade River at White Swan Hotel (Michelin), halal dim sum, vegetarian options. Essential dish guide, tea selection, 50–150 RMB/person.

- **[architectureontheroad-shamian-island-guide.md](architectureontheroad-shamian-island-guide.md)** — "Shamian Island: Colonial Architecture & History" (architectureontheroad.com). Opium Wars history, British/French concession, 150+ preserved buildings (HSBC, Our Lady of Lourdes), Neoclassical/Gothic/Baroque styles, walking tour, connections to Qingping Market and Xiguan.

- **[echinacities-guangzhou-markets-shopping-guide.md](echinacities-guangzhou-markets-shopping-guide.md)** — "Guide to Guangzhou's Markets" (echinacities.com). Six markets: Qingping (1,200 booths), Hualin Jade, Fangcun Tea, Wende Culture Street, Haizhu Square, Zhanxi Fashion. Bargaining tips, hours, souvenir guide.

- **[mytravelation-guangzhou-day-trips-guide.md](mytravelation-guangzhou-day-trips-guide.md)** — "8 Day Trips from Guangzhou" (mytravelation.com). Kaiping Diaolou UNESCO towers, Foshan martial arts/ceramics (metro-accessible), Qingyuan hot springs, Zhaoqing Seven Star Crags, Shunde UNESCO gastronomy city, Conghua hot springs, Pearl River cruise.

- **[tripzilla-guangzhou-first-timer-guide.md](tripzilla-guangzhou-first-timer-guide.md)** — "7-Day Guangzhou Itinerary" (tripzilla.com). Day-by-day: Sacred Heart Cathedral, Chen Clan Academy, Yongqingfang, Canton Tower, Shenzhen/Macau day trips. Restaurant picks (Dian Dou De, Rong Hua Lou). Xihua Road budget dining, hanfu photoshoot options, visa-free entry.
