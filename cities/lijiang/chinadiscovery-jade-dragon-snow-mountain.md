# Jade Dragon Snow Mountain (玉龙雪山) — Complete Visitor Guide, Lijiang

**Source:** China Discovery — chinadiscovery.com/yunnan/lijiang/jade-dragon-snow-mountain.html

## Quick Facts

- **Chinese name:** 玉龙雪山 (Yùlóng Xuě Shān) — "Jade Dragon Snow Mountain"
- **Location:** 15 km north of Lijiang Old Town
- **Highest peak:** Shanzidou (扇子陡), 5,596 m — never summited
- **Peak count:** 13 peaks forming a dragon-like ridge
- **Highest accessible point:** Glacier Park at 4,680 m (via cable car)
- **Climate note:** Snow coverage decreasing due to global warming
- **Best months:** January–February, April (clearest skies); November–April for snow
- **Avoid:** July–August (rainy, clouds obscure mountain)

## Three Cable Car Systems (三条索道)

### Glacier Park Cable Car (冰川公园索道) — Summit Experience
- **Top station:** 4,506 m; boardwalk reaches 4,680 m
- **Cable car price:** CNY 120 per person (round trip)
- **What you see:** Active glaciers, blue-green serac pillars ("Green Snow Peaks" 绿雪奇峰), panoramic high-altitude views
- **Boardwalk:** Paved path from cable car station to highest viewpoint (~30 min, go slow)
- **Hours:** 06:00–18:00 (May–Oct); 06:30–18:00 (Nov–Apr)
- **Altitude warning:** 4,500+ m — oxygen cans recommended; rent down jacket at base

### Spruce Meadow Cable Car (云杉坪索道)
- **Top station:** ~3,000 m
- **Price:** CNY 67 round trip
- **What you see:** Alpine meadow surrounded by ancient spruce forest, snow mountain backdrop
- **Character:** Peaceful, flat walking; good for those avoiding extreme altitude
- **Best for:** Families, photography, avoiding crowds

### Yak Meadow Cable Car (牦牛坪索道)
- **Top station:** ~3,700 m
- **Price:** CNY 62 round trip
- **What you see:** Broad grassland with grazing yaks, Tibetan-style herdsmen, wildflowers
- **Character:** Pastoral landscape; fewer visitors; seasonal beauty
- **Best for:** Quiet experience, cultural interaction with herders

## Blue Moon Valley / White Water River (蓝月谷/白水河)

- **Location:** At the base of the mountain, between Spruce Meadow and Glacier Park
- **What:** Series of terraced pools fed by snowmelt
- **Color:** Milky blue-white on sunny days (white marble lakebed); deeper blue when cloudy
- **Trail:** Flat boardwalk connecting multiple pools; ~1 hour walking
- **Free to visit** with mountain entrance ticket
- **Best photography:** Late morning when sun illuminates the pools

## Impression Lijiang Show (印象丽江)

- **Director:** Zhang Yimou (张艺谋) — famed Chinese filmmaker
- **Venue:** Ganhaizi outdoor theater at 3,100 m altitude
- **Performers:** 500+ local minority performers against the mountain backdrop
- **Showtime:** 13:30 daily (off-season); 12:50 and 14:20 (peak season)
- **Price:** CNY 190–248 per person depending on seating
- **Duration:** ~70 minutes
- **Content:** Naxi, Bai, Yi, and Tibetan ethnic music, dance, and rituals
- **Tip:** Dress warmly; open-air venue at altitude can be cold even in summer

## Altitude Sickness Prevention (高原反应预防)

- **Glacier Park is above 4,500 m** — mild to moderate altitude symptoms common
- **Oxygen cans:** Available for purchase at base and cable car stations (~CNY 30–60 each); buy 1–2
- **Down jacket rental:** Available at base station (~CNY 50) for those without warm layers
- **Prevention tips:**
  - Do not rush at altitude; walk slowly on boardwalk
  - Stay hydrated; avoid alcohol the night before
  - Eat a light breakfast
  - If headache or nausea worsen, descend immediately
- **Most visitors are fine** with basic precautions and oxygen can

## Getting There from Lijiang

- **Distance:** 15 km north of Lijiang Old Town
- **By tour bus:** From Hongtaiyang Square (红太阳广场); departs before 07:30; returns before 17:00; CNY 20 round trip
- **By taxi/private car:** 30–40 minutes; CNY 80–120
- **By organized tour:** Most popular option; includes transport, guide, tickets
- **Tip:** Arrive early (before 08:00) to beat crowds at Glacier Park cable car — waits can exceed 2 hours in peak season

## Suggested Day Plan

1. **07:00** — Depart Lijiang Old Town
2. **07:30** — Arrive at scenic area; purchase tickets
3. **08:00** — Glacier Park cable car up; explore boardwalk at 4,680 m (1.5 hours)
4. **10:30** — Descend; take shuttle to Blue Moon Valley (1 hour walking)
5. **12:00** — Lunch at mountain base restaurants
6. **13:30** — Impression Lijiang show (optional; 70 min)
7. **15:30** — Spruce Meadow cable car (optional; 1 hour)
8. **17:00** — Return to Lijiang

## Practical Tips

- **Book Glacier Park cable car tickets in advance** during peak season (Trip.com, Klook, or Chinese platforms)
- **Go on a clear day** — check weather forecast; mountain is often cloud-shrouded
- **Wear layers** — base at 3,000 m is mild; summit at 4,500+ m is freezing even in summer
- **Sturdy shoes** for boardwalks and Blue Moon Valley trails
- **Photography:** Best mountain shots from Lijiang Old Town's Black Dragon Pool or Lion Hill; best close-up from Glacier Park boardwalk
- **Allow a full day** — combining Glacier Park, Blue Moon Valley, and the show takes 8+ hours
- **Budget CNY 500–800** per person for entrance + cable cars + show + transport
