# Dali First-Timer Itinerary & Practical Guide (大理初次旅行指南)

**Source:** China Highlights — chinahighlights.com/dali/how-to-plan-a-trip.htm

## Quick Facts

- **Location:** Dali Bai Autonomous Prefecture (大理白族自治州), western Yunnan Province
- **Altitude:** ~2,000 m (Dali Old Town)
- **Climate:** Mild year-round, 10–25 C; strong UV; large daily temperature swings
- **Best months:** March–May (flowers, festivals), September–November (clear skies, mild)
- **Also good:** Mid-October–December (warm sunshine, quiet, fewer crowds)
- **Rainy season:** July–August (brief afternoon showers)
- **Recommended duration:** 3–4 days for highlights; 5–7 days for deeper exploration

## Getting to Dali

### By High-Speed Train (高铁)
| From | Duration | Frequency |
|------|----------|-----------|
| **Kunming (昆明)** | 2–2.5 hours | Multiple daily |
| **Lijiang (丽江)** | 2–2.5 hours | Multiple daily |
| **Shangri-La (香格里拉)** | ~4 hours | Limited service |

- Dali Railway Station (大理站) is in Xiaguan New City, ~30 min taxi to Old Town
- **Tip:** Book tickets on 12306 app or Trip.com; purchase 2–3 days ahead in peak season

### By Flight
| From | Duration |
|------|----------|
| Kunming | 1 hour |
| Chongqing | 2 hours |
| Guangzhou | 3 hours |
| Shanghai | 4 hours |
| Beijing | 4 hours |

- Dali Airport (大理机场/DLU) is in Fengyi Town, ~45 min taxi to Old Town
- Fewer flights than Kunming or Lijiang airports

### By Private Car
- Kunming to Dali: ~4.5 hours by expressway
- Lijiang to Dali: ~3 hours
- Useful if adding Shaxi Ancient Town (沙溪) en route

## Suggested Itineraries

### 3-Day Essential Dali
**Day 1: Dali Old Town (大理古城)**
- Walk the main streets: Renmin Road, Foreigner Street (护国路)
- South Gate (南门) photo stop
- Climb to Wuhua Tower (五华楼) for rooftop views
- Evening: Bar street or night market

**Day 2: Cangshan Mountain (苍山)**
- Morning: Cable car up (Zhonghe or Gantong) + Jade Belt Road hike
- Afternoon: Bai tie-dye cultural experience at Zhoucheng Village
- Evening: Return to Old Town

**Day 3: Erhai Lake (洱海)**
- Full-day cycling or e-bike loop (east or west shore)
- Stop at Xizhou for Baba flatbread and Three-Course Tea
- Flower cake-making workshop (optional)

### 4-Day Itinerary (add)
**Day 4:** Shuanglang Old Town (双廊) — lakeside boutique village, Nanzhao Folk Island

### 5–7 Day Deep Exploration (add)
- **Shaxi Ancient Town (沙溪):** Ancient Tea Horse Road trading post; Friday Market; 2.5 hours from Dali
- **Shibaoshan Buddhist Caves (石宝山):** Ming-era Buddhist grottoes near Shaxi
- **Jianchuan Old Town (剑川):** Woodcarving capital of Yunnan
- **Nuodeng Village (诺邓):** 1,000-year-old salt-producing village; famous Nuodeng ham

## Where to Stay

### Dali Old Town (大理古城) — Most Popular
- Lively nightlife, restaurants, cafes; walkable to everything
- **Luxury:** Honglongjing Hotel, The One Resort
- **Boutique:** Wucaiyun Garden Hotel
- **Budget:** Dali Landscape Hotel
- **Price range:** CNY 150–800/night

### Erhai Lake Shoreline — Best Views
- Quieter, scenic lakefront properties
- **Luxury:** Hotel Indigo Dali Erhai (洱海英迪格)
- **Mid-range:** Munwood Panorama Resort, Manshan Yise
- **Price range:** CNY 300–1500/night

### Xizhou Ancient Town (喜洲) — Cultural Immersion
- Traditional Bai village atmosphere; fewer tourists than Old Town
- **Luxury/Boutique:** The Linden Centre (喜林苑)
- **Price range:** CNY 200–1200/night

### Shaxi Ancient Town (沙溪) — Off the Beaten Path
- Overnight stopover for Tea Horse Road exploration
- **Boutique:** Shaxi Landscape Hotel, Sunyata Shaxi Hotel

## Getting Around Dali

- **Within Old Town:** Walking (compact, flat)
- **Erhai Lake loop:** Rent e-bike (CNY 60–100/day) or bicycle (CNY 30–50/day)
- **To Xizhou/Zhoucheng:** Bus CNY 7, taxi CNY 40, or cycle
- **To Shuanglang:** Bus or taxi (40 min, CNY 30–80)
- **To Cangshan cable cars:** Bus routes 4 or 9, taxi CNY 15–20
- **To Shaxi:** Minibus from Dali North Bus Station (~2.5 hours)

## Cultural Experiences

- **Bai tie-dye workshop** in Zhoucheng: ~2 hours, CNY 50–100
- **Flower cake making** (鲜花饼): Arranged through guesthouses, ~1 hour
- **Three-Course Tea ceremony** at Xizhou's Yan Family Compound
- **Morning market** at Xizhou or Old Town South Gate (arrive before 08:00)
- **TV drama locations:** "Meet Yourself" (去有风的地方) filmed in Dali — Fengyangyi Village

## Connecting Routes Through Yunnan

### Classic Yunnan Triangle (8–10 days)
**Kunming → Dali (3 days) → Lijiang (2–3 days) → Shangri-La (2 days)**

### Extended Western Yunnan
**Dali → Shaxi → Lijiang → Tiger Leaping Gorge → Shangri-La → Meili Snow Mountain**

### Southern Route
**Dali → Kunming → Stone Forest → Jianshui → Yuanyang Rice Terraces**

## Practical Tips

- **Sun protection is non-negotiable** — altitude + reflection means sunburn risk even on cloudy days; bring SPF 50+, hat, sunglasses
- **Layer clothing** — temperatures can swing 15 C between morning and afternoon
- **Cash still useful** — WeChat Pay/Alipay dominate but some village vendors are cash-only
- **Altitude is mild** at 2,000 m — most visitors feel no effects; drink extra water as precaution
- **Book trains early** in peak season (Chinese holidays, July–August, October Golden Week)
- **"Wind, Flowers, Snow, Moon"** (风花雪月) — Dali's famous poetic description: Xiaguan wind, Shangguan flowers, Cangshan snow, Erhai moonlight
- **Dali is a slow-travel destination** — resist over-scheduling; budget time for wandering and cafe sitting
