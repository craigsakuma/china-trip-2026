# Cangshan Mountain (苍山) — Hiking & Cable Car Guide, Dali, Yunnan

**Source:** China Discovery — chinadiscovery.com/yunnan/dali/cangshan-mountain.html

## Quick Facts

- **Chinese name:** 苍山 (Cāng Shān) — "Green/Azure Mountain"
- **Location:** West of Dali Old Town, parallel to Erhai Lake
- **Peak count:** 19 peaks running north–south, all above 3,500 m
- **Highest peak:** Malong Peak (马龙峰), 4,122 m
- **Peaks above 4,000 m:** 7
- **Streams:** 18 named streams flowing east toward Erhai Lake
- **Distance from Dali Old Town:** ~1.9 km from Cangshan Gate (苍山门)
- **Best months:** March–May (azaleas, camellias), September–November (clear skies, autumn color)

## The Three Cable Car Systems (三条索道)

### Ximatan Cableway (洗马潭索道) — Summit Access
- **Top elevation:** 3,920 m (highest cable car in the Cangshan system)
- **Round-trip price:** CNY 282
- **Hours:** 08:00–17:00
- **Duration:** ~30 minutes each way
- **What you see:** Alpine meadows, snow remnants, panoramic Erhai Lake views
- **Note:** Weather-dependent; may close for high winds or low visibility

### Zhonghe Cableway (中和索道) — Mid-Mountain
- **Top elevation:** ~2,600 m (Zhonghe Temple area)
- **Round-trip price:** CNY 90–100; one-way CNY 60; hiking-only ticket CNY 35 (off-peak CNY 20)
- **Hours:** 09:00–16:00
- **Best for:** Access to Jade Belt Road (玉带路) hiking trail; quietest of the three

### Gantong Cableway (感通索道) — Southern Access
- **Top elevation:** ~2,600 m
- **Round-trip price:** CNY 80
- **Hours:** 08:00–17:00
- **Best for:** Most attractions clustered nearby (Gantong Temple, waterfalls, caves)

## Hiking Trails

### Jade Belt Road / Yudai Road (玉带路)
- **Length:** 18 km (11 miles) one-way
- **Elevation:** ~2,600 m, relatively flat traverse
- **Duration:** 5–6 hours for full length
- **Route:** Winds around six peaks, passes five streams, connects three temples
- **Key sights along trail:** Longyan Cave (龙眼洞), Zhonghe Temple (中和寺), Qilongnv Pool (七龙女池), Phoenix Eye Cave (凤眼洞)
- **Difficulty:** Moderate; well-maintained stone path
- **Access:** Take Zhonghe or Gantong cable car up, hike along, descend via the other

### Jade Cloud Road (玉带云游路)
- **Length:** 11.5 km
- **Duration:** ~5–6 hours
- **Route:** Passes Dragon Stream, Yuju Peak, Green Jade Stream, Longquan Peak
- **Character:** Similar to Jade Belt Road but different sections; stone path with forest cover

### Cangshan Summit Approach
- **Via Ximatan Cableway** to 3,920 m, then boardwalks and short trails
- **What to see:** Washing Horse Pool (洗马潭), alpine flowers (May–June)
- **Altitude warning:** Above 3,500 m — take it slow, watch for altitude symptoms

## Key Temples and Sites

### Zhonghe Temple (中和寺)
- Mid-mountain temple accessible via Zhonghe Cableway
- Panoramic viewpoint over Erhai Lake and Dali Old Town
- Starting/ending point for Jade Belt Road

### Gantong Temple (感通寺)
- Historic Buddhist temple near southern cable car
- Adjacent to Seven Dragon Pool waterfall area
- Gantong Tea served here — local specialty

### Phoenix Eye Cave (凤眼洞)
- Natural cave formation along Jade Belt Road
- Named for eye-shaped opening that frames Erhai Lake

## Flora and Biodiversity

- **Azaleas (杜鹃花):** Over 40 species; bloom spectacularly at 3,500–4,100 m in May–June
- **Camellias (山茶花):** Dali's city flower; peak bloom February–April
- **Orchids, rhododendrons, primulas** throughout mid-elevations
- **Dense coniferous forests** on north-facing slopes; alpine meadows above treeline

## Weather and What to Bring

| Altitude Zone | Conditions | Gear |
|--------------|-----------|------|
| **Base (2,000 m)** | Mild year-round, 10–25°C | Light layers, sun hat |
| **Mid (2,600 m)** | Cooler, variable; fog possible | Fleece/jacket, rain shell |
| **Summit (3,900+ m)** | Cold, windy, UV intense | Warm layers, gloves, sunscreen SPF 50+ |

- Weather changes rapidly at elevation — always carry rain gear
- Sturdy walking shoes with grip essential for stone paths
- Carry 1–2 liters of water; limited purchase points on trails

## Getting to Cangshan from Dali Old Town

- **Walk:** ~30 minutes from South Gate or Cangshan Gate
- **Bus:** Routes 4 or 9 to cable car stations
- **Taxi:** CNY 15–20 to any cable car station
- **Bicycle:** Ride to base, park, then cable car up

## Practical Tips

- **Combine cable cars:** Take Zhonghe up, hike Jade Belt Road, descend via Gantong (or reverse) for the best one-day experience
- **Start early** (before 09:00) for clearest mountain views; clouds roll in by afternoon
- **Altitude caution:** If coming directly from low elevation, spend a day acclimatizing in Dali (2,000 m) before hiking above 3,500 m
- **Best photography:** Sunrise/early morning from Zhonghe Temple looking east over Erhai; sunset illuminates the peaks from the lake side
- **Budget half-day minimum** for any cable car + short walk; full day for Jade Belt Road traverse
