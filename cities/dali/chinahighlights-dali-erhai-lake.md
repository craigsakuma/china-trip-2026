# Erhai Lake (洱海) — Complete Visitor Guide, Dali, Yunnan

**Source:** China Highlights — chinahighlights.com/dali/attraction/the-scenery-of-erhai-lake.htm

## Quick Facts

- **Chinese name:** 洱海 (Ěr Hǎi) — "Ear-Shaped Sea"
- **Location:** Dali Bai Autonomous Prefecture, Yunnan Province
- **Elevation:** 1,972 m above sea level
- **Dimensions:** ~41 km long, 3–9 km wide, ~120 km circumference
- **Significance:** Second-largest highland freshwater lake in Yunnan; "Pearl of the Plateau"
- **Best months:** March–May, September–October (mild, scenic); winter for Siberian gulls

## Cycling Around Erhai Lake (洱海环湖骑行)

The full loop is approximately 120 km (75 miles), typically done over 1–2 days. Two main shore routes:

### Haixi Route (West Shore 海西)
- **Start:** Cai Village (才村), near Dali Old Town
- **End:** Xiaguan District (下关)
- **Distance:** ~13 km one-way (partial section)
- **Character:** Motor vehicles prohibited; flat and safe for inexperienced riders
- **Scenery:** Cangshan Mountain backdrop, farmland, Bai villages

### Haidong Route (East Shore 海东)
- **Best section:** Wase Village (挖色) to Wenbi Village (文笔)
- **Distance:** ~13 km one-way
- **Character:** Panoramic mountain-and-lake views; quieter than west shore
- **Tip:** Ride in the afternoon to catch sunset over Cangshan

### Bike Rental
- Available throughout Dali Old Town and lakeside towns
- Shared bikes accessible via phone app
- Helmets provided; e-bikes popular for full loop

## Boat Cruises on Erhai Lake

### Standard Cruise Route
- **Duration:** ~4 hours
- **Departure piers:** Xiaguan Pier (Erhai Park), Longkan Pier (near Old Town), Taoyuan Pier
- **Price:** CNY 140–180 per person
- **Includes:** Three Tea Ceremony performance, song-and-dance show, Nanzhao-style island stop

### Independent Boat Hire
- Smaller boats available at Shuanglang and Wase for short rides
- Negotiate prices locally; typically CNY 30–80 for short trips

## Key Towns and Villages Around Erhai Lake

### Shuanglang Old Town (双廊古镇) — Northeast Shore
- Bluestone walls, narrow alleys, boutique guesthouses
- **Key sites:** Yuji Island (玉几岛), Nanzhao Folk Island (南诏风情岛)
- Popular sunset-watching spot; increasingly touristy

### Xizhou Ancient Town (喜洲古镇) — West Shore
- 17 km north of Dali Old Town
- Famous for Bai traditional architecture and Xizhou Baba (喜洲粑粑) flatbread
- Morning market, Three-Course Tea ceremony, tie-dye workshops

### Wase Village (挖色镇) — East Shore
- Quiet fishing village with Bai-style ancient gate towers (late Qing/early Republic era)
- Authentic local atmosphere; excellent for lakefront accommodation
- Less commercialized than Shuanglang

### Haidong (海东) — East Shore
- Small Bai ethnicity town with three shallow bays
- Jinsuo Island (金梭岛) accessible by small boat

## Islands and Temples

### Little Putuo Island (小普陀)
- Tiny island on the east shore with Guanyin Pavilion (观音阁)
- One of the best spots for watching Siberian black-headed gulls (November–March)
- Iconic photo spot; boats ferry visitors from shore

### Nanzhao Folk Island (南诏风情岛)
- Accessible via cruise or Shuanglang ferry
- Reconstructed Nanzhao-kingdom architecture and cultural exhibits

## Best Time to Visit

| Season | Conditions | Highlights |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| **Spring (Mar–May)** | Mild, flowers blooming | Best overall; soft breezes, clear skies |
| **Summer (Jun–Aug)** | Warm, occasional downpours | Green landscapes; strong UV |
| **Autumn (Sep–Oct)** | Cool, wonderful light | Golden hour photography; harvest scenes |
| **Winter (Nov–Feb)** | Cool but sunny | Siberian gulls at Little Putuo; fewer crowds |

## Getting to Erhai Lake from Dali Old Town

- **Cai Village (east gate to lake):** ~3 km, 10-minute bike ride or short taxi
- **Shuanglang:** 40 minutes by bus or taxi (~CNY 80)
- **Xizhou:** 30 minutes by bus (CNY 7) or taxi (~CNY 40)

## Practical Tips

- **Sun protection essential** — high altitude + lake reflection = intense UV year-round
- **Carry water and snacks** when cycling remote east-shore sections
- **E-bikes recommended** over traditional bicycles for the full loop (saves 1 day)
- **Accommodation:** Lakeside guesthouses in Shuanglang and Wase offer best water views
- **Photography:** Sunrise from east shore looking west at Cangshan; sunset from west shore
- **Weather changes fast** — pack a light rain jacket even in dry season
