# Getting to Fenghuang Ancient Town: Transport & Logistics Guide

**Source:** China Discovery — chinadiscovery.com/hunan/fenghuang-ancient-town/how-to-get.html

## Fenghuang's Location

Fenghuang Ancient Town (凤凰古城) is located in Fenghuang County, Xiangxi Tujia and Miao Autonomous Prefecture, western Hunan Province. It is not directly on a major rail line but has its own high-speed station opened in recent years. The nearest major cities are Zhangjiajie (to the north), Changsha (to the east), and Guiyang (to the southwest).

## Fenghuanggucheng Railway Station (凤凰古城站)

The dedicated high-speed rail station is approximately 10 km (20 minutes drive) from the old town center. Direct train connections include:

| Destination | Duration | Notes |
|-------------|----------|-------|
| Zhangjiajie | ~1 hour | Most common connection |
| Changsha | 2-3 hours | From Changsha South or Changsha Station |
| Guangzhou | ~5 hours | Direct high-speed trains |
| Shanghai | ~8 hours | Direct service |
| Beijing | ~9.5 hours | Direct service |
| Wuhan | ~4 hours | Direct service |
| Shenzhen | ~5 hours | Direct service |

## From Zhangjiajie (张家界) to Fenghuang — Most Common Route

### High-Speed Train (Recommended)
The most efficient and most chosen option for independent travelers. Bullet trains depart from Zhangjiajie West Station (张家界西站) to Fenghuanggucheng Station. Duration approximately 1 hour. Trains run more than twenty times daily, almost hourly from early morning until evening. Tickets: 93 RMB (second class), 149 RMB (first class), 326 RMB (business class).

### Bus
Duration: 3.5-4 hours. Distance approximately 240 km. Earliest departure 8:50 AM. Tickets around 90 RMB. Less convenient than the train but cheaper.

### Private Car
Around 200 km via expressway. Takes 3-4 hours depending on traffic. Useful if traveling with luggage or in a group.

## From Changsha (长沙) to Fenghuang

### High-Speed Train (Primary Option)
- **From Changsha South Station (长沙南站):** Approximately 2-2.5 hours
- **From Changsha Station (长沙站):** Approximately 3 hours
- Distance: approximately 410 km

### Bus
Usually 2 runs per day. Duration approximately 6 hours. Not recommended when train is available.

## From Fenghuanggucheng Station to the Old Town (10 km)

### Maglev Train (磁悬浮列车)
A short magnetic levitation train connects the station to the town area.
- **Hours:** 9:00 AM - 9:00 PM
- **Price:** 48 RMB one-way / 68 RMB round-trip
- Somewhat overpriced for the short distance

### Shuttle Bus
- **Price:** 7 RMB per person
- **Routes:** To Qifeng Square Tourist Center (奇峰广场游客中心) or Nanhuangli Tourist Center (南华里游客中心)
- The most budget-friendly option

### Taxi
- **Price:** approximately 25 RMB
- **Duration:** approximately 20 minutes
- **Note:** Ride-hailing apps (Didi) may not be available at this station. Regular taxis line up outside.

### Private Car / Hotel Shuttle
Many hotels offer pickup services if arranged in advance. Recommended for convenience, especially with luggage.

## Airport Access

### Tongren Fenghuang Airport (铜仁凤凰机场, Guizhou Province)
- **Distance:** approximately 40 km
- **Drive time:** approximately 40 minutes
- **Direct flights from:** Beijing (2.5 hours), Shanghai (2 hours), Nanjing (4 hours)
- **No international flights**

### Alternative Airports
- **Huaihua Zhijiang Airport (怀化芷江机场):** approximately 90 km away
- **Zhangjiajie Hehua International Airport (张家界荷花国际机场):** approximately 200 km away — useful if combining with Zhangjiajie visit

## Getting Around Within Fenghuang

**On foot (best option):** The old town is compact with narrow cobblestone streets. Walking is the primary and best way to explore. Everything within the ancient town core is within 15 minutes on foot.

**Tourist shuttle bus:** 28 RMB for a 3-day unlimited pass. Connects various entry points and the wider town area.

**River boats:** Daytime and nighttime Tuojiang River cruises provide views of the stilt house groups from the water.

**Didi / ride-hailing:** Works in the newer parts of town outside the ancient core. Not practical within the old town streets.

## Recommended Travel Sequence

The most popular and logical itinerary combining Fenghuang with other western Hunan destinations:

### Option A: Zhangjiajie First, Then Fenghuang
1. Fly into Zhangjiajie (or train from Changsha)
2. Spend 2-3 days at Zhangjiajie National Forest Park
3. Optional: Day trip to Furong Town (25 min train)
4. Train to Fenghuang (1 hour)
5. Spend 1-2 nights in Fenghuang
6. Train to Changsha (2.5 hours) for departure

### Option B: Changsha -> Fenghuang -> Zhangjiajie
1. Arrive Changsha
2. Train to Fenghuang (2.5 hours)
3. 1-2 nights in Fenghuang
4. Train to Zhangjiajie (1 hour)
5. 2-3 days Zhangjiajie
6. Depart from Zhangjiajie

### Recommended Duration
- **Fenghuang old town only:** 1-2 days
- **Fenghuang + one Miao village:** 2 days
- **Full western Hunan circuit (Zhangjiajie + Furong Town + Fenghuang):** 5-6 days

## Practical Tips

- Book train tickets in advance during Chinese holidays (Golden Week, Spring Festival) — trains sell out
- The Fenghuanggucheng station is relatively new and well-organized
- Consider arriving in the afternoon and experiencing the town at night first
- Luggage storage available at hotels and some tourist centers
- Last trains from Fenghuang depart in the evening — check schedules carefully if day-tripping
- The maglev is a novelty but the shuttle bus at 7 RMB is perfectly adequate
