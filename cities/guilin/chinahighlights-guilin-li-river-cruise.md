# Li River Cruise from Guilin to Yangshuo (漓江游船): 5 Ways to Enjoy the Best Scenery

**Source:** China Highlights — chinahighlights.com/guilin/attraction/li-river.htm

| Detail | Info |

## Cruise Types & What to Expect

### 3-Star Boats
Standard tourist vessels seating ~120 passengers. Simple boxed lunch included. Open upper deck for photography. Adequate for budget travelers who want the core Li River experience.

### 4-Star Boats (Recommended)
Premium vessels with only 75-80 seats, leather sofas, buffet dining. ~$20 more than 3-star. Significantly less crowded, better photo opportunities from uncrowded upper deck. Worth the upgrade for October travel.

### Booking Tips
- Peak-season tickets sell out fast — book at least 1 month in advance
- 9:00 AM departure slot offers optimal morning light for photography
- Hotel concierge or local booking saves vs. international travel platforms
- Passport required for ticket purchase (foreign travelers)

## Scenic Highlights Along the Route (漓江精华段)

### Yangdi to Xingping Section (杨堤-兴坪)
The most spectacular 24 km stretch, featuring the densest concentration of karst peaks:

- **Crown Cave (冠岩)** — Early highlight with illuminated underground river
- **Half-Side Ferry (半边渡)** — Narrow river passage between towering cliffs
- **Nine-Horse Fresco Hill (九马画山)** — 100m cliff face where locals spot 9 horse shapes in the rock patterns; legend says spotting all 9 brings fortune
- **Yellow Cloth Shoal (黄布倒影)** — Featured on China's 20-yuan banknote; best reflections in calm morning water. The riverbed's yellow stone creates the name
- **Xingping Ancient Town (兴坪古镇)** — 1,700-year-old town, disembarkation point for bamboo raft alternative

## 5 Ways to Experience the Li River

### 1. Full Cruise (Guilin to Yangshuo)
The classic 83 km, 4-hour journey covering all highlights. Best for first-time visitors wanting the complete panorama.

### 2. Bamboo Raft: Yangdi to Xingping (杨堤-兴坪竹筏)
Motorized bamboo rafts through the most scenic 1.5-hour section. More intimate than cruise ships, closer to water level. ~¥216/raft (seats 4). Age restriction: 7-70 years old.

### 3. Bamboo Raft: Yangdi to Nine Horses (杨堤-九马画山)
Shorter 1-hour section focusing on the dramatic cliff formations. Good for time-limited visitors.

### 4. Xingping to Nine Horses Raft
Short 40-minute raft covering Yellow Cloth Shoal and Nine-Horse Fresco Hill — the two most iconic landmarks.

### 5. Yulong River Bamboo Raft (遇龙河竹筏)
Quieter tributary near Yangshuo. Hand-poled (not motorized) bamboo rafts through rural countryside. More peaceful, fewer tourists, beautiful bridge reflections. Multiple sections available at different price points.

## Best Seasons & Weather

| Season | Conditions | Photography |
|--------|-----------|-------------|
| **Spring (Mar-May)** | Misty rain, green peaks, moderate temps | Atmospheric mist shots; moody reflections |
| **Summer (Jun-Aug)** | Hot, clear skies, highest water | Blue-sky karst panoramas; lush greenery |
| **Autumn (Sep-Nov)** | Mild, crisp air, golden light | Best overall: clear + golden hour glow |
| **Winter (Dec-Feb)** | Low water, possible fog | River may be too low for full cruise |

**October (trip month):** Excellent conditions — mild temperatures, clear skies, golden autumn light. Post-National-Day crowds thin after Oct 7.

## Post-Cruise: Arriving in Yangshuo (阳朔)

After disembarking at Yangshuo, options include:
- **Bicycle countryside tour** — Rent bikes and ride along Yulong River to ancient bridges
- **West Street (西街)** — Pedestrian street with bars, restaurants, souvenir shops
- **Stand-up paddleboarding** on Yulong River
- **Cooking class** at Yangshuo Cooking School

## Practical Tips

- **What to bring:** Sunscreen, hat, rain jacket (weather changes fast), camera with telephoto lens for cliff details
- **Photography:** Morning departure means front-lit eastern cliffs; shoot from upper deck left side
- **Food:** 4-star buffet is decent; 3-star boxed lunch is basic — consider bringing snacks
- **Motion:** River is calm; motion sickness unlikely
- **Return transport:** Bus from Yangshuo back to Guilin (~1.5 hours, frequent departures)
- **Pier arrival:** Allow extra time for the 50-min drive from Guilin hotels to pier
