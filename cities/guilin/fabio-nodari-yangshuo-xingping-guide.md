# Yangshuo and Xingping Travel Guide

**Source:** Fabio Nodari (fabionodariphoto.com), travel photographer based in China. Published Oct 14, 2025. Updated Feb 8, 2026. All first-hand experience.

## Quick Facts

- **Location:** Guangxi Province (广西). Guilin (桂林) is the regional hub; Yangshuo (阳朔) and Xingping (兴坪) are the scenic heart with iconic karst hills.
- **Landscape:** Towering limestone karst peaks, Li River (漓江), Yu Long River (遇龙河). Most famous karst photos are from Yangshuo/Xingping, not Guilin city.
- **Best time:** November, December, January (clear skies, Pomelo harvest in Nov). Avoid summer (hot, humid, high season) and Chinese New Year (mid-Jan to mid-Feb).
- **Typical stay:** 3–5 days to cover Guilin, Yangshuo, and Xingping properly.

## Choosing a Base: Guilin vs Yangshuo vs Xingping

**Guilin (桂林):** Famous but touristy with little landscape value. Most iconic karst photos are NOT from Guilin. Good for: Li River cruise start, Reed Flute Cave (芦笛岩), Elephant Trunk Hill, Longsheng/Longji Rice Terraces (龙胜梯田/龙脊梯田). Three train stations: Guilin Bei (桂林北站), Guilin Xi (桂林西站), Guilin (桂林站). Airport 30 km from center.

**Xingping (兴坪):** Most beautiful of the three. Least touristy. Scene on 20 RMB banknote. Most spectacular stretch of Li River. Cormorant fishermen. Stay at least 2 nights.

**Yangshuo (阳朔):** Surrounded by spectacular karst mountains. More developed (West Street has Starbucks, nightclubs, souvenirs). If choosing only one, stay in Yangshuo.

**Fabio chose NOT to visit Guilin** due to limited time and stayed in Yangshuo and Xingping instead.

## Xingping (兴坪): Highlights

**Xianggong Hill (相公山):** Famous sunrise viewpoint. Arrive at least 1 hour before sunrise for a tripod spot — fierce competition with Chinese photographers. Arrange driver through your hotel: expect 150+ RMB round trip. Entrance fee for the steep stairs (30 min to top).

**Li River ferry:** 5 RMB, 5 minutes to cross. Cheap alternative to expensive river cruise.

**Cormorant fishermen:** Photo sessions available for a fee. Part of local cultural heritage.

**Li River cruise:** Fabio does NOT recommend — overpriced and not authentic. But available via GetYourGuide if desired.

**Getting there:** The "Yangshuo" HSR station is actually located in Xingping — 20-minute fast train from Guilin. Take bus or taxi from station.

**Where to stay:** Guesthouse on hills outside town recommended — photographer owner built a mountain viewpoint path, wife is excellent cook. Peaceful, like going back in time. Downside: no public transport access, owner drives you to town.

## Yangshuo (阳朔): Highlights

**West Street:** Main tourist area with all the usual shops, hostels, nightclubs. Contrasts with surrounding landscape.

**Moon Hill:** 8 km from town. Bike there (~1 hour) or take taxi. Hill with crescent moon hole. 800+ steps to climb. Bike rental: 10–20 RMB/day.

**Yu Long River Bike Route (遇龙河):** Fabio's top recommendation. Start from Tea Cozy hotel in Xiatang village (夏棠寨). Ride north along Yu Long River to Fuli Bridge (副里桥). Lunch at Secret Garden restaurant (English-speaking staff, Western and Chinese dishes, in a characteristic village with few tourists). Continue to Yulong Bridge Scenic Area, cross river, ride south on west bank through villages where you'll likely be the only tourist.

**Bamboo rafts:** ~300 RMB. Fabio found them "quite boring and also expensive."

**Impression Sanjie Liu Show:** Spectacular performance on shores of Li River.

**Where to stay:** Tea Cozy hotel in Xiatang (夏棠寨), in the Yulonghe Scenic Area (遇龙河景区). Area closed to non-resident traffic. Nice hotel in Chinese countryside.

## Getting Around: Guilin to Yangshuo

**Bus:** From Guilin Central Station (10 min from main train station). Direct and local buses every ~20 min. Direct bus: ~1.5 hours. Best and cheapest option.

**From Guilin airport:** Direct buses every ~1.5 hours, 9:30–22:30. About 1.5 hours to Yangshuo bus station. Airport is ~30 km from Guilin center, ~80 km from Yangshuo.

**Private driver:** Guilin to Yangshuo: 350+ RMB.

**Note:** "Yangshuo" train station is in Xingping, NOT Yangshuo. From station you need bus or minibus to Yangshuo town.

## Practical Tips

- **Taxis:** Expensive in Guilin/Yangshuo (100 RMB for a few km). Use Didi (Chinese Uber) to save ~30%.
- **Google Maps:** Not very accurate in China. Use AMAP instead.
- **VPN:** Essential. Download before arriving.
- **eSIM:** Recommended.
- **Best month:** November is optimal — clear skies, comfortable weather, Pomelo harvest season.
- **Accommodation costs:** Range from budget hostels to luxury. Book on Trip.com for best deals.
