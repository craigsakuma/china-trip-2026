# Traveler Profile

## Group Overview

- **Age range:** Early 50s
- **Travel style:** Prefer authentic, local experiences over touristy attractions
- **Activity level:** Adventurous and active for their age — enjoy hiking
- **Food:** Enjoy good food, open to local cuisine
- **Accommodation:** Mid-range to boutique/unique stays preferred; open to splurging once or twice for something special
- **Evenings:** Enjoy night markets, casual bars, and live music; not into clubbing or heavy drinking. Don't need to go out every night — culture over nightlife
- **Cultural immersion:** High priority — want to experience local life, not just see sights
- **Asia experience:** Craig lived in Shanghai ~20 years ago; speaks minimal Mandarin. Others — TBD
- **Language:** Very limited Mandarin in the group
- **Budget:** Comfortable spending for quality experiences; not extravagant
- **Dietary:** No restrictions — everyone open to trying anything. Note: Kiran is squeamish about seeing whole animals (duck heads, shrimp heads, etc.) but eats all meats
- **Pace:** Active — prefer full days with a rest day after every 1-2 packed days. Will push through for must-do experiences
- **Interests:** Art, history (with a knowledgeable guide — not generic tours), hiking, food
- **Nice to have:** Cooking class (low priority)
- **Guide preference:** Strong preference for expert/knowledgeable guides over generic tour groups
